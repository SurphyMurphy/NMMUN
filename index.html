F.A.Q

  • Is there a registration fee?

    Yes, there is a registration fee of 20BHD for all delegates and chairs.

  • Will food be provided on the day of the conference?

    We will provide breakfast, lunch, and snacks on the days of the conference.

  • Can I sign up if I know nothing about MUNs?

    Yes you can! We will provide all delegates with training sessions and our team will always be available to assist you.

  • When do applications close?

    Chair applications close on the 23rd of September 2022 and delegate applications close on the 13th of October 2022.

  • What is the dress code for NMMUN?

    All MUN Directors and participants should dress appropriately when the conference is in session. Males should be in suits with jackets and ties, and must remain in the jacket while speaking. Females should be in dresses and skirts of reasonable length or smart pants. Footwear should also match the occasion and shirts should be appropriate and formal.
