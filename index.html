About The Event

The New Millennium Model United Nations is an opportunity to break free from the vapid state of the mind and engage in stimulating dialogue and exchange of views, with the ultimate goal of producing transformative, provoking and rousing ideas. The event models the structure of the United Nations by introducing issues concerning the present climate. Participants partake in the event as delegates , along with Chairs who look into the operation and orderly functioning of their respective committees. And with committees, issues and explanations galore, we are certain of a productive and worthwhile event that will let everyone involved come out feeling a little bit of a revolutionary.
